The Lemonade Stand Dream

The Lemonade Stand Dream: What’s a Business?

Have you ever imagined selling the yummiest cookies or the most refreshing lemonade on a sunny day? Guess what — that’s the beginning of a business!

A business is simply:

  • Making or doing something people want (like tasty lemonade, cool greeting cards, or even walking dogs!)
  • And in return, they give you money 💵

It’s about solving a problem or making someone’s day brighter.

A Lemonade Stand Example

Picture this: You set up a small table outside your home

  • You’ve got lemons 🍋
  • Sugar 🍬
  • Cups 🥤
  • And a big smile 😃

People passing by stop, buy your lemonade, and leave happy. That’s your first taste of running a business!

Business Ideas for Kids Like You 💡

You are not into lemonade? That’s okay! Think about what people around you might enjoy or need:

  • Greeting Cards – Create colorful cards that make people smile 🎨
  • Garden Helper – Offer to water plants or tidy up gardens 🌱
  • Pet Care – Walk the neighbor’s dog or play with pets 🐶

Remember: A business can be big or small — it’s all about helping people while having fun.

🧠 Smart Kid CEO Tip:

“Every great business starts with a simple idea — even a glass of lemonade!”

Activity Time! (Get your tablet or a book and pen)✍

Draw your dream business by answering these questions

  • What will you sell?
  • Who will you help?
  • How will it make people happy?

👏 Congrats! You just discovered what a business really is. This is your first step toward becoming a Mini CEO!
